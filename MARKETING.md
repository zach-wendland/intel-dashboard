## Growth Tactics

### Week 1: Validation
- Post on Twitter, Gab, Truth Social
- Share in relevant Reddit communities
- Post in Telegram groups (America First groups, MAGA channels)
- **Goal:** 100 email signups

### Week 2: Iterate
- Survey users: "What would make you pay $9.99/month?"
- Add most-requested features
- **Goal:** 3% of users say "yes" to premium

### Week 3-4: Pre-Launch Hype
- Daily countdown to premium launch
- Showcase premium features (GIFs, videos)
- "Last chance for 50% lifetime discount"
- **Goal:** 200 waitlist signups

### Month 2: Launch Premium
- Email waitlist with payment link
- Post launch announcement
- **Goal:** 2-5% conversion (4-10 paid users from 200 waitlist)

### Month 3-6: Scale
- Affiliate partnerships with streamers
- Sponsor placement on small podcasts
- SEO content (blog posts on trending topics)
- **Goal:** 1,000 free users, 30-50 paid ($300-500 MRR)

## Messaging Guidelines

### Always Emphasize:
- **Freedom:** "No censorship, no algorithms"
- **Convenience:** "All your patriot content in one place"
- **Independence:** "Built by patriots, for patriots"
- **Trust:** "No tracking, no data selling"

### Never Say:
- "Alt-right" (media smear term)
- "Extremist" (defensive posture)
- "Hate speech" (accepting leftist framing)

### Tone:
- Confident, not apologetic
- Aggressive against establishment, friendly to users
- Data-driven when possible ("30+ sources", "6 RSS feeds")
- Authentic, not corporate

## KPIs to Track

### Week 1:
- Social media impressions
- Click-through rate to site
- Email signup rate
- Time on site

### Month 1:
- Daily active users
- Retention (7-day, 30-day)
- Email open rates
- Waitlist signups

### Month 2+:
- Free-to-paid conversion rate
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Churn rate
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Lifetime value (LTV)
